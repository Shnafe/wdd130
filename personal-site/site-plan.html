Overview

Purpose

Pitch:

Do you ever feel like you aren`t progressing in life? I have experienced that same feeling, which is why I decided to make “The Quest Board.” “The Quest Board” works just like a video game where you do tasks which give you XP when completed. My goal is to get you to start achieving your goals by incentivizing them with the same system that makes video games so rewarding to our brains: XP and Leveling. You can write your own tasks (or quests) and put them on the board, or if you are having trouble thinking of goals you would like to achieve, we have some basic ones already there for you. All you need to do is accept them and they will be added to your board.

THIS IS THE WEBSITE FOR THE APP. IT HAS INFO AND FAQs ON THE APP. IT ALSO HAS A SUPPORT PAGE. IT TALKS ABOUT ANY UPCOMING UPDATES.

APP INFO | Home Page

This page will contain information on the app including any recently added features. It will also contain any additional future update info. This is where people will go when they have questions or concerns about the recent updates to the app. This page will also contain screenshots and videos of the app being used.

FAQs

This page will contain any frequently asked questions so people can quickly and easily find answers to their basic questions. If a user has a more complex question and cannot find the answer on the FAQs, they should check out the support page.

Support

This page is for users who have questions that go beyond the FAQs. This is also where users can request support if anything isn't working as it should.

Audience

The audience is mostly people who don't get out much, and nerds who have trouble making and keeping goals. We all know that nerds rule the world, but only the nerds who actually make and accomplish goals.
